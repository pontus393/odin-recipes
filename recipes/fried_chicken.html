Fried Chicken

Description

You'll love these tender fried chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Frying oil
  • Seasoning
  • Eggs
  • Corn Flakes

Steps

  • Heat up the oil in a large pan
  • Dip the chicken pieces in eggs
  • Roll the chicken in crushed Corn Flakes
  • Fry
  • Add seasoning
  • Enjoy
