Tacos

Description

Fantastic tasting tacos. A favorite of mine, for sure.

Ingredients

  • Taco bread
  • Beef
  • Seasoning
  • Tomatoes and sallad
  • A cheese of your tasting

Steps

  1. Start by chopping up the beef and then fry it along with the seasoning
  2. Start cutting the sallad
  3. Once the meet is well-done, make the tacos - don't forget the cheese
