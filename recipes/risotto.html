Risotto

Description

Really tasty. Don't cut down on the cheese.

Ingredients

  • Risotto rice
  • Parmigiano
  • Bouillon
  • Salt
  • Dry white wine

Steps

>
  1. Fry the rice until its golden
  2. Add some white wine
  3. Add 2 deciliters of buillon, add again when dried up until the rice has good texture
  4. Add the parmigiano
